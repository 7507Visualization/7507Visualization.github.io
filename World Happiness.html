Factors Influencing Happiness Score

What might be some of the driving forces behind a person' happiness? Here are some key factors to explain the variation:
Healthy life expectancy, Social support: Freedom to make life choices, Generosity, Perceptions of corruption and some environmental factors.
Positive affect is defined as the average of three positive affect measures in GWP: happiness, laugh and enjoyment.
Negative affect is defined as the average of three negative affect measures in GWP: worry, sadness and anger.
